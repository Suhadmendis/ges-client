Graduate Diploma in Business Management

Home courses Graduate Diploma in Business Management

  • This qualification aims to help students to develop a critical understanding of management in the context of general business.
  • The qualification will give you a theoretical understanding of management concepts and an overview of general business. It will equip you to find employment with management prospects in general business or to continue your professional or academic studies in a related area.

    • Candidates who have passed any Level 5 Diploma are eligible to take this qualification. Other applicants must have obtained one of the following:
  • A UK Degree or Overseas equivalent. BTEC HND in business management or related Studies.
  • Other level 5 qualifications from another internationally recognized awarding body or approved Overseas Institutes of Higher Education.
